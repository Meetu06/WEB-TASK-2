Asset Protection

National Capital Security Group provides bespoke security services solutions for all types of facility security, infrastructure security, construction site security, and building site security. Our security professionals have received training in areas like as customer service, workplace health and safety, dispute de-escalation and resolution, and more. We will conduct this training every quarter for all workers assigned to your location or facility, utilising our online learning management system. Our security professionals can dress in one of our distinctive security uniforms, a black suit with a white shirt, or a client/organization-specific attire. Our security guards are equipped with high-visibility vests and other safety gear.

Some of the services we provide for facility security include:

  • Office Building Security Guards
  • Industrial Building Security Guards
  • Gatehouse Security Services
  • Residential Apartment Security Officers
  • Highrise Building Security Officers
  • Strata Security Services
  • Industrial Complex Security Services
  • Construction Site Security Guards

CCTV Operations

  • We perform both reactive and preventative maintenance for CCTV systems as well as provide and install them. We deploy CCTV monitoring and response systems to assist defend the safety and integrity of your business, as well as the wellbeing of your employees and clients, as well as public liability, asset and property protection.
  • When compared to traditional physical security systems, our team of highly qualified Control Center operators and CCTV/Alarm Technicians will provide you the peace of mind you need to run your business successfully while saving you money.
  • Our stringent CCTV Code of Conduct, which is part of the introduction process for all CCTV operators, governs all CCTV monitoring.
  • Our expert experts will develop a maintenance and service schedule to guarantee that your security system runs smoothly 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

CUSTOMER SERVICE OFFICERS

The concierge, front-of-house, and customer service workers at National Capital Security Group are well dressed and create a cheerful and welcoming atmosphere. When it comes to hiring, it's critical to understand the function and select the ideal sort of individual for it. After all, these personnel will be our clients' brand ambassadors. To achieve this, our specialised HR staff frequently recruits people with customer service or hospitality expertise from outside the security field, and then provides them with the required security training.

CONTROL ROOMS

National Capital Security Group personnel monitor site security systems, building management systems and closed circuit television systems (CCTV). These personnel complete specific training, which includes the operation, monitoring and testing of alarm panels, security cameras, monitoring systems and communications.

ACCESS CONTROL

Security officers who ensure the integrity of controlled access points by denying entry of all unauthorised personnel. Key elements include registering visitors, production of access and photo identification cards, conducting identification checks and searches. Comprehensive guidelines are followed for differing security threat levels.

FIRE WARDENS

National Capital Security Group Security regularly makes its security officers available to be in emergency response teams, either as chief, deputy or area fire wardens. Additional training or drills are completed following a strict schedule.

MOBILE PATROLS

Mobile security patrols are an effective way to protect your assets, without the cost of a full-time security guard. We provide our mobile patrol guards with comprehensive training and ensure they are familiar with your site so they can respond quickly to irregularities and disturbances.

Our mobile security patrol services and mobile patrol guards include:

  • Perimeter patrols
  • Site inspections
  • Alarm response
  • Emergency response
  • Patrol vehicles
