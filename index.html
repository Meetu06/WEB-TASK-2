National Capital Security Group

Welcome to National Capital Security Group. At National Capital we are proud to provide complete security services to a range of national organisations including large commercial enterprises, e property management businesses, financial institutions, hotels, licensed clubs, logistic companies, and the public sector.

Asset Protection

National Capital Security Group provides bespoke security solutions for all types of physical assets including event facilities, infrastructure, commercial premises, construction sites, and domestic homes. Our security professionals have received comprehensive training in customer service, workplace health and safety, dispute de-escalation and resolution, and more.

CCTV Operations

We supply and install video surveillance systems. We perform reactive and preventative maintenance for close circuit television (CCTV) systems. We deploy CCTV monitoring and response systems to assist defend the safety and integrity of your business, ensuring the wellbeing of your employees and clients, as well as asset and property protection.

Concierge and Customer Servicen

National Capital Security Group believes that providing outstanding customer experience requires a personal touch. Our concierge and customer service guards are professional, friendly, and approachable, ensuring that your customers have a positive and lasting impression. Our concierge, front-of-house, and customer service workers are well dressed and create a cheerful and welcoming atmosphere.

Loss Prevention Officers

National Capital Security Group can design and implement solutions to help clients reduce their losses and, as a result, increase their profits.

Mobile Patrols & Event Responses

We conduct random inspections of unattended assets while ensuring the most efficient response to duress and alarm activations using cutting-edge despatch, navigation, and reporting technology.

Control Rooms

National Capital Security Group personnel monitor site security systems, building management systems and CCTV systems. These personnel are specially trained in the operation, monitoring and testing of alarm panels, security cameras, monitoring systems and communications.

Access Control

Security officers may ensure the integrity of controlled access points by denying entry of all unauthorised personnel. Key elements include registering visitors, production of access and photo identification cards, conducting identification checks and searches. Comprehensive guidelines are followed for differing security threat levels.

Fire Wardens

National Capital Security Group Security officers are available to be in emergency response teams, either as chief, deputy or area fire wardens. Additional training or drills are completed following a strict schedule.

Mobile Patrols

Mobile security patrols are an effective way to protect your assets, without the cost of a full-time security guard. We provide our mobile patrol guards with comprehensive training and ensure they are familiar with your site so they can respond quickly to irregularities and disturbances.
